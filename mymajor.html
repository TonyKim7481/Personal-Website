Major: IST Enterprise Technology Integration


Choosing Enterprise Technology Integration (ETI) as my major was a clear choice for me because it sits at the exciting crossroads of technology and business. In ETI, I'm learning to analyze organizational needs and implement tech solutions that propel businesses forward. It's thrilling to think about being the link between complex IT systems and strategic goals, preparing me for a dynamic future in any industry. The prospect of being in high demand and having diverse career paths available only adds to the appeal. This major is about making an impact and solving real-world challenges, which is exactly what I want to do.
