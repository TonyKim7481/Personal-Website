Activities

Anime Club


I joined the anime club to connect with others who share my interest in anime's storytelling and art. It's a great space to discover new series and engage in meaningful discussions about our favorite aspects of the genre. This club offers a sense of community and a chance to deepen my appreciation for anime alongside fellow enthusiasts.

Korean Student Association


Joining the Korean Student Association at Penn State offered me a sense of community and connection to my heritage while studying abroad. It's a place to share and embrace cultural experiences, navigate challenges together, and maintain a link to South Korea, all of which enrich my time at Penn State.

Art Club


I joined the art club as a budding artist to immerse myself in a creative environment, surrounded by fellow art enthusiasts. It's a space where I can refine my skills, exchange feedback, and draw inspiration from diverse artistic perspectives. The club offers opportunities to showcase my work, participate in workshops, and explore various art forms, fueling my passion and growth as an artist.
